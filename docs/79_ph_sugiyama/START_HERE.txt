THE BUG IN ONE SENTENCE:
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

We're storing parent_folder="src" but should store parent_folder="src/api/routes"


THE FIX IN ONE LINE:
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

File: scripts/rescan_project.py
Line: 392

CHANGE:
  parent_folder = rel_path.split(os.sep)[0]
TO:
  parent_folder = os.path.dirname(rel_path)


WHY THIS MATTERS:
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  All 2088 files treated as siblings under "src"

  Appearance: "Mash" ❌

After Fix:
