## Research Response

[Model A]
To resolve the layout overlap issue in the VETKA parallax playground, we must transition from a "floating/overlay" UI model to a structured vertical stack within the central workspace. This ensures the preview (Stage) has its own dedicated screen real estate, while control panels (Workflow/Manual) are relegated to a scrollable area beneath it.

1. Files to Modify
photo_parallax_playground_codex/photo_parallax_playground/src/index.css: To define the grid/flex hierarchy.
photo_parallax_playground_codex/photo_parallax_playground/src/App.tsx: To restructure the JSX hierarchy and move panels into the new containers.
2. Implementation Approach

The strategy involves refactoring the middle column (.main-pane) into a "Professional Monitor" layout:

Stage-First Constraint: The top section of the middle column is reserved exclusively for the preview.
Workflow Deck: A dedicated scrollable container at the bottom of the middle column for the high-density controls (Depth, Extract, Camera, Routing).
Removal of Overlays: Any panels currently using position: absolute or fixed that cover the center must be moved into the static flow of the Workflow Deck.
