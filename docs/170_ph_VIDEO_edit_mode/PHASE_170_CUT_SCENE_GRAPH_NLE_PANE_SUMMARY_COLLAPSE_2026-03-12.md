# PHASE 170 ŌĆö CUT Scene Graph NLE Pane Summary Collapse

## Goal
Reduce clutter in the NLE Scene Graph pane when needed without hiding the viewport or core actions.

## Collapse Rule
- `Selection Summary` and `Media Card` are secondary and may collapse.
- `Viewport Priority` stays visible.
- `Actions` stay visible.

## Visible Signals
- toggle: `Hide Details` / `Show Details`
- collapsed copy: `secondary summaries collapsed`
