Interrupt + Restart Stream Protocol:

1. **Stop Stream**: When the interrupt protocol is triggered, the current stream is stopped.
2. **Clean-up**: The voice handoff is cleaned up, and the tool-loop is paused.
3. **Restart Stream**: A new stream is started, and the voice handoff is re-established.
