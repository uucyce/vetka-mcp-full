# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard
We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive environment.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and specific in technical feedback.
- Focus on issues, not people.
- Collaborate in good faith.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or contribution flow.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove or reject contributions that violate this policy.
