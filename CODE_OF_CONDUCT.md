# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical issues, not personal attacks.
- Accept feedback and collaborate in good faith.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, threats, or discrimination.
- Trolling, doxxing, or repeated disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private/confidential information without consent.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors for violations.

## Reporting
Report conduct issues to project maintainers via private channel.
